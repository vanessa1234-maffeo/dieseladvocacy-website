About us

Diesel Advocacy Pty Ltd is an Australian private company, incorporated in New South Wales in 2025. We design and build digital health products grounded in the lived realities of our users — starting with people undergoing cancer treatment and the families who support them. Our work prioritises privacy, dignity, and clinical credibility.

Diesel Advocacy is owner-operated, headquartered in regional New South Wales, and bound by Australian privacy law including the Privacy Act 1988 and the 13 Australian Privacy Principles.
